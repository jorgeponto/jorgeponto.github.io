The following Success Criteria are new in WCAG 2.2:

- [Accessible Authentication](https://www.w3.org/TR/WCAG22/#accessible-authentication)
- [Dragging](https://www.w3.org/TR/WCAG22/#dragging)
- [Findable Help](https://www.w3.org/TR/WCAG22/#findable-help)
- [Fixed Reference Points](https://www.w3.org/TR/WCAG22/#fixed-reference-points)
- [Focus Appearance (Minimum)](https://www.w3.org/TR/WCAG22/#focus-appearance-minimum)
- [Focus Appearance (Enhanced)](https://www.w3.org/TR/WCAG22/#focus-appearance-enhanced)
- [Hidden Controls](https://www.w3.org/TR/WCAG22/#hidden-controls)
- [Pointer Target Spacing](https://www.w3.org/TR/WCAG22/#pointer-target-spacing)
- [Redundant Entry](https://www.w3.org/TR/WCAG22/#redundant-entry)

The new success criteria may reference new terms that have also been added to the glossary and form part of the normative requirements of the success criteria.

In addition to the above new Success Criteria, [Focus Visible](https://www.w3.org/TR/WCAG22/#focus-visible) has been promoted from Level AA to Level A.
